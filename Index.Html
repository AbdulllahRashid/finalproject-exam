Societal Mission

Air University aspires to be among the leading national universities, excelling in teaching, learning, research, innovation and public service.
Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and
contribution with integrity; advancing fundamental knowledge and cultivating
creativity; leading in pioneering research for effective clinical therapies; and
accelerating solutions and amplifying their impact.
